Classic Creamy Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs Yukon Gold or Russet potatoes, peeled and quartered 3 lbs Yukon Gold or Russet potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 cup whole milk (warm)
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp salt (plus more to taste)
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional, for garlic mashed potatoes)
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (optional: parsley or chives)

Instructions

  1. Boil the potatoes
    Place potatoes in a large pot, cover with cold salted water, and bring to a boil. Cook for 15–20 minutes until fork-tender.
  2. Warm the milk and butter
    In a small saucepan, gently heat the milk and butter until melted and warm (do not boil).
  3. Mash the potatoes
    Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Mash until smooth using a potato masher or hand mixer.
  4. Make it creamy
    Slowly pour in the warm milk and butter mixture while mixing until creamy and fluffy.
  5. Season and serve
    Add salt, pepper, and optional garlic. Taste and adjust seasoning. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.

Why You’ll Love It

  • ✔ Creamy texture
  • ✔ Crowd-pleasing classic
  • ✔ Perfect with gravy, turkey, and stuffing
  • ✔ Easy to make ahead for Thanksgiving prep
